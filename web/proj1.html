ABOUT ME

HI! I’M MARIEL ANONUEVO, A FRONT-END WEB DEVELOPER WHO LOVES CREATING WEBSITES THAT ARE BOTH BEAUTIFUL AND EASY TO USE. I ENJOY TURNING IDEAS INTO INTERACTIVE WEB PAGES USING HTML, CSS, AND JAVASCRIPT. FOR ME, FRONT-END DEVELOPMENT ISN’T JUST ABOUT CODING—IT’S ABOUT DESIGNING EXPERIENCES PEOPLE CAN CONNECT WITH.

I LOVE THE CREATIVE SIDE OF WEB DEVELOPMENT, FROM CHOOSING COLORS AND LAYOUTS TO MAKING SURE EVERYTHING LOOKS SMOOTH AND RESPONSIVE ON ANY DEVICE. I ALWAYS AIM TO MAKE EVERY WEBSITE CLEAN, FAST, AND ENJOYABLE TO USE.

OUTSIDE OF CODING, I LIKE EXPLORING WEB DESIGNS AND FINDING INSPIRATION FROM DIFFERENT WEBSITES. I BELIEVE EVEN SMALL DETAILS CAN MAKE A BIG DIFFERENCE, AND I’M ALWAYS EXCITED TO KEEP IMPROVING AND BRING NEW IDEAS TO EVERY PROJECT.
